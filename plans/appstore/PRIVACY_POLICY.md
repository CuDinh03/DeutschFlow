This version of the app contains no advertising and no in-app purchases.

### 3. Device permissions

We ask for a permission only when a feature needs it, and each is optional:

- **Microphone** — to record your speech for pronunciation and speaking practice.
- **Camera / Photos** — to let you photograph or upload written assignments as images.
- **Notifications** — to send study reminders and app notifications.

You can change or revoke any permission at any time in **iOS Settings → DeutschFlow**. Revoking a permission disables the related feature but does not otherwise affect the app.

### 4. How we use your data

We use the data above to: operate the app and your account; grade your work and generate AI feedback; personalize your learning path and spaced-repetition schedule; send the reminders you opted into; prevent fraud and abuse and keep accounts secure; and analyze aggregate usage to improve DeutschFlow. Our legal bases (where GDPR applies) are performance of our contract with you, your consent (for notifications), and our legitimate interest in improving and securing the service.

### 5. Third parties and sub-processors

We share personal data only with the service providers that help us run the app, each acting under its own terms and only as needed:

- **PostHog** (United States region) — product and usage analytics.
- **Expo / EAS** — delivers push notifications and over-the-air app updates.
- **Amazon Web Services (AWS)** — cloud hosting for our backend, which stores your account and learning data.

We do not share your data with any other third parties except where required by law.

### 6. Data retention and deletion

We keep your personal data while your account is active. **You can permanently delete your account and all associated learning data at any time, directly in the app: Profile → Delete account.** This is immediate and irreversible. You can also request deletion by emailing [[support@your-domain]] from your registered address. After deletion, we may retain a limited set of records where the law requires it, and de-identified analytics that can no longer be linked to you may be kept.

### 7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights over your personal data:

- **EU / EEA / UK (GDPR / UK GDPR):** access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and the right to withdraw consent.
- **California (CCPA / CPRA):** know, access, delete, correct, and opt out of "sale"/"sharing" (we do not sell or share personal data as those terms are defined).
- **Vietnam (PDPD — Decree 13/2023/ND-CP):** access, correct, delete, withdraw consent, and object to processing.

You can exercise most of these rights in the app (edit your profile, or Delete account). For anything else, contact [[support@your-domain]] and we will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law. You also have the right to complain to your local data-protection authority.

### 8. Children

DeutschFlow is intended for a general audience and is **not** directed to children under 13. The app is **not** enrolled in Apple's Kids Category. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact [[support@your-domain]] and we will delete it.

### 9. Security

We protect your data with industry-standard measures: passwords are stored only as salted hashes; authentication tokens are stored in the device's secure keychain; and all traffic uses HTTPS with App Transport Security enforced. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work continuously to protect your data.

### 10. International data transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than the one you live in — for example, analytics in the United States and cloud hosting on AWS. Where we transfer data internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) as required by applicable law.

### 11. Changes to this policy
