# DeutschFlow

DeutschFlow là nền tảng học tiếng Đức trực tuyến được tối ưu cho tốc độ tiến bộ và hiệu quả ghi nhớ. Hệ thống kết hợp **comprehensible input**, **spaced repetition (SM-2)**, **early AI speaking**, và **retrieval practice** để đưa người học từ A1 lên B1 trong một lộ trình 12 tuần có cấu trúc rõ ràng.

## Roadmap 12 tuần

### Phase 1 — Foundation (Weeks 1-4, 32 hours)
- A1 vocabulary: 200-300 words
- Grammar: articles, present tense through input
- Speaking: greetings and introductions
- AI Speaking: from day 1
- SRS: from day 1

### Phase 2 — Production (Weeks 5-8, 36 hours)
- A2 vocabulary: 500 words total
- Grammar: past tense, cases through speaking
- Writing: sentence completion to paragraphs
- Interview prep starts

### Phase 3 — Fluency (Weeks 9-12, 38 hours)
- B1 vocabulary: 700 words total
- Interview mode: 5 phases
- Mock CEFR B1 test
- Graduation: B1 confirmed

## Learning science

- **Comprehensible Input:** learners understand most of the content from the start
- **SM-2 SRS:** intervals of 1, 3, 7, 14, 30 days target long-term retention
- **Interleaving:** vocab, grammar, and speaking are mixed from week 1
- **Retrieval Practice:** speaking and writing are used to reinforce input
- **Contextual Grammar:** grammar is learned as patterns in context
- **Early Speaking:** AI practice starts on day 1 instead of week 7

## Exit criteria

By the end of week 12, the learner should be able to:
- retain 700 words with 90%+ recall
- hold a 25-30 minute conversation
- use major tenses and cases with 85%+ accuracy
- feel comfortable making mistakes while speaking
- pass a B1-level CEFR confirmation check
